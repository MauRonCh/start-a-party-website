About

I Wanna Start A Party is a fangame of I Wanna Be The Guy inspired by the Mario Party saga.

You can play it alone or together with friends with its online mode!

This game is being developed mainly by KingSlendy with the help of many others.

It has different modes to play, and many collectables to get!

  • Party
  • Minigames
  • Trials
  • Store

Play in a board, collect a lot of Shines and become the party star!

Section where you can enjoy playing all the 40 minigames in the game

Different minigames packed together in all sort of wacky ways!

Buy all sorts of different stuff to spice up the game!
